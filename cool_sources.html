This is a collection of websites I find cool and useful

They are roughly divided into subcategories

Learning

  • Typing.com Increase the bandwidth between you and your computer.
  • Missing Semester (a bunch of basic stuff put together by some M.I.T. profs)
  • Learn markdown in 10 mins looks like it's not going away or getting changed anytime soon, like txt files, unlike the everchanging glorious MS docx files
  • rwxrob personal website Boost/Skilstak has a lot of goodies (specially for tmux, bash, vim, ssh and lynx quick searching)
  • Khan Academy One of the best websites for learning anything in general

Many of these things if not all I use for note taking and I regard them as basic knowledge everyone should have

Books

  • Z library Most likely has the book you are looking for (link on this page might not work where you are at so google for you local mirror of this site)

    • note: if book limit download reached use a temp mail service to make an account and if book not available in through regular internet you might have to use tor protocol to access it

  • Librivox Has a lot of audiobooks but isn't very easy to search (uses torrent protocol for sharing files)
  • Standardebooks Best for old books that are out of copyright and for EPUB books, they have the best text formatting I've seen
